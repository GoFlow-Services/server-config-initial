Why You Should Clean Your Air Ducts

Your air ducts act like the lungs of your home with dirt, dust, dander, hair, oils, grease, and chemicals getting recirculated through your home on average of 5-7 times each day. A clean HVAC system promotes a healthier indoor environment, contributing to added productivity, comfort, and a sense of well-being.

According to the EPA, the air quality in your home can be anywhere from 2-5 times worse than the air quality outside your home. In fact, the EPA and its Science Advisory Board consistently rank indoor air pollution as one of the top five environmental health risks to the public.
