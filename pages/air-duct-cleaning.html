How Often Should HVAC Systems be Cleaned?

According to the National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA), most homes benefit from a professional air duct cleaning every 2 to 5 years. The right schedule depends on your home and lifestyle, but you should consider cleaning your air ducts if:

  • You have pets that shed a lot of hair or dander.

  • You’ve recently moved into a new home.

  • You’ve done any renovations or remodeling

  • Someone in your household has allergies or asthma.

  • There’s been water damage or contamination in your HVAC system.

  • You’ve installed or serviced new HVAC equipment.

  • You’ve noticed pests, insects, or rodents in your ductwork.
