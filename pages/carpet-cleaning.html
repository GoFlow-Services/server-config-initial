Benefits of Carpet Cleaning

Improved Indoor Air Quality

Your carpet quietly collects dust, allergens, and everyday pollutants that can affect the air you breathe. Over time, these build up deep within the fibers. GoFlow’s professional carpet cleaning service clears away these hidden contaminants, restoring freshness and improving your home’s indoor air quality.

Reduced Risk of Allergies and Asthma

If you or your loved ones struggle with allergies or asthma, your carpet could be part of the problem. GoFlow’s cleaning system lifts away the dust and pet dander that can trigger reactions, leaving your home healthier and easier to breathe in.

Boosted Immune System

The buildup of dirt, dust, and other pollutants in your carpet can also impact your immune system. When your immune system is weakened, you become more susceptible to illness and disease.

GoFlow Carpet Cleaning Process

Our carpet cleaning involves all four industry standards of Chemistry, Heat, Agitation, and Time (CHAT) to leave nothing but clean carpets behind.

✓

Step 1
Pre-Treating & Agitating

After applying a pre-treatment spray, our counter-rotating brushes gently scrub and refresh your carpet fibers, loosening and lifting dirt, debris, hair, and soil from the carpet fibers, stuff your vacuum can't reach.

✓

Step 2
Deep Clean with Engineered Water

We use a high-pH, ultra-clean water solution (no detergents, no sticky residue) to lift out deep-down dirt, oils and grime — then flush it away. You get fresher carpets, faster dry time and no residue left behind.”

✓

Step 3
Rinsing & Drying

We use pressurized clean water and a powerful extractor to pull out dirt, oils, and moisture without over-wetting your carpet. Then, our air movers speed up airflow so your carpets dry fast and stay fresh.

✓

Step 4
Protecting

Our optional protector spray acts as an invisible barrier against dirt and spills, preventing them from soaking or embedding into your carpet's fibers.

How often should you clean your carpets?

Most carpet manufacturers require you to clean your carpets at least once every 12-18 months to maintain the warranty. But, this may not be enough if you've got a busy household.

The frequency of vacuuming and evidence of spots should determine how often your carpets need cleaning; not the calendar.

Carpet is a surface that adds softness, warmth, and luxury to rooms in your home. At GoFlow, we clean your carpets in a way that leaves them looking newer longer.
