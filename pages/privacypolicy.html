Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

At GoFlow Building Services, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, request a quote, or use our services in Saskatoon and surrounding areas.

1 Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with our cleaning services, process quotes, and communicate with you. This may include:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, and service address
  • Service details: information about your home or property relevant to your cleaning request
  • Website data: cookies or analytics to understand website performance and improve user experience

2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide estimates, quotes, and cleaning services
  • Schedule appointments and confirm bookings
  • Communicate updates and follow-up services
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Comply with legal and accounting requirements

We do not sell or share your personal information with outside companies for marketing purposes.

3 Consent

By submitting your information through our website, quote form, or by phone/email, you consent to our collection and use of your data as outlined in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

4 Data Storage & Security

We store all customer data securely using encrypted systems and password-protected software. Only authorized GoFlow staff and contractors who need access to perform their duties can view this information.

5 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party platforms (such as Google Ads, Facebook, or Jobber) to manage bookings and advertising. These services have their own privacy policies, and we ensure they comply with applicable privacy standards in Canada.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Request details about how your data is being used
  • Withdraw consent for communications

To make a request, email us at allan@goflowservices.com

7 Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or Google Analytics to track traffic and performance. Cookies help us understand which pages are most useful to visitors so we can improve our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.
